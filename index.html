A Boardwalk Tradition

For over 25 years, Polish Water Ice has been a staple of the Wildwood boardwalk. What started as a small family stand has grown into a beloved destination for locals and visitors alike.

We make every batch by hand using real fruit, pure cane sugar, and filtered water. No artificial colors, no shortcuts — just the good stuff.
